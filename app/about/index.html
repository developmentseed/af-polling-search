Votehere.af

VOTEHERE.AF is a project enable Afghan citizens to find their nearest polling center to vote in the Presidential and Provincial Council Elections on April 5, 2014. Use VOTEHERE.AF to search for the nearest polling center and the approximate distance.

Data updated March 31, 2014.
